Why read a novel when you have Netflix?

Since we'reall sick of talking about red states vs. bue states, let's take a break
and switch to a different metric. How about book states vs. mobile states? Or
dance states vs. museum states?

Those sort of breakdowns are found in a pair of studies just realeased by the
National Endowment for the Arts, wich track participation in the arts on
both the national and state level. Though the research includes some fun
findings, it also confirms at least one troubling trend: We're increasingly less
likely to read literature.

According to the NEA, the share of adults who report reading literature has
steadily fallen in recent years, from 47 percent in 2012 to 45 percent n 2013
and 43.1 percent in 2015
